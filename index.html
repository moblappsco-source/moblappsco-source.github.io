About Us

Mobl Games is an independent studio building approachable puzzle games with strong art direction, satisfying gameplay loops and a warm, modern brand feel.

Our goal is to combine thoughtful design, accessible gameplay, and charming visuals to create games that feel rewarding from the first tap.

We like keeping things elegant and readable, so players can relax, explore and enjoy the experience without friction.

Every detail is tuned to feel polished, playful and memorable, from the illustrations to the pacing.
