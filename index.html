About Us

Mobl Games is an independent game studio focused on creating relaxing and enjoyable puzzle experiences for players around the world.

Our goal is to combine thoughtful design, accessible gameplay, and charming visuals to build games that people can enjoy anytime, anywhere. We believe great games do not need to be complicated, they should be easy to pick up, satisfying to play, and memorable long after the session ends.

With a passion for creativity and continuous improvement, we are dedicated to crafting high-quality mobile games that bring moments of fun and relaxation to everyday life.
